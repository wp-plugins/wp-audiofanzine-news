== Description ==

This widget allows you to include Audiofanzine's news feed on your Wordpress website, to provide news about musical instruments and audio gear to your visitors.
Filter the news feed according to the subject that is the most relevant to you : Computer Music, Recording, Guitar, Bass, Drums, Electronic instruments, DJing or Lights. And thanks to the multilingual versions of Audiofanzine, choose between its 6 languages : english, french, german, italian, japanese and spanish.

== Installation ==

1. Upload 'wpafnews' folder to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Edit widget options in 'Appearance > Widgets'
4. See Audiofanzine's last news on your own website !
